Unicode Stress Test Document
========================================

1. Curly quotes and apostrophes:
   “Dit is een test” en ‘dit ook’
   He said “Hello” and she replied ‘Hi’

2. Non-breaking spaces and soft hyphens:
   Woord met nbsp (non-breaking spaces)
   Lang­woord­met­zachte­af­bre­kingen (soft hyphens)

3. Combining characters:
   é (e + combining acute) vs é (precomposed)
   ñ (n + combining tilde) vs ñ (precomposed)

4. Mixed language with special chars:
   Dutch: Coördinatie, financiële, geïnteresseerd
   German: Größe, Müller, Straße
   French: Ça va, Père Noël, façade
   English: naïve, résumé, café

5. Currency and symbols:
   € 1.234,56 | £ 2,345.67 | ¥ 10,000 | $ 99.99
   ½ ¼ ¾ | © ® ™ | → ← ↑ ↓ | • ◦ ▪

6. Zero-width characters:
   Word​with​zero​width​space (invisible breaks)
   Word‌with‌zwnjhere (zero-width non-joiner)

7. Emoji in text:
   Status: ✅ Gereed | ⚠️ Waarschuwing | ❌ Fout
   Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
